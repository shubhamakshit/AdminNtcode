About Taekwondo

Taekwondo is a Korean martial art characterized by its emphasis on high and fast kicks. It combines combat techniques, self-defense, sport, and exercise.

Taekwondo Belts

Taekwondo uses a belt system to indicate the rank and progress of practitioners. The belt colors typically range from white to black, representing beginner to advanced levels.

Taekwondo Forms

Forms, also known as "poomsae" or "patterns," are a series of prearranged movements that simulate a fight against multiple opponents. Practicing forms helps improve technique and balance.
